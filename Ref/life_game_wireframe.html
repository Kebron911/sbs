üé® Design Specifications

Color Palette
  • #3498db - Primary Blue (buttons, active states)
  • #2ecc71 - Success Green (good habits, completed tasks)
  • #e74c3c - Danger Red (bad habits, HP damage)
  • #f39c12 - XP Gold (rewards, coins)
  • #9b59b6 - Epic Purple (rare items)
  • #2c3e50 - Dark Gray (primary text)
  • #ecf0f1 - Light Gray (backgrounds, borders)
Typography
  • H1 Page Title: 24px, Bold (700)
  • H2 Section Title: 20px, Bold (700)
  • Body Text: 14px, Regular (400)
  • Small Text: 12px, Regular (400)
  • Button Text: 14px, Bold (700)
Spacing & Layout
  • Screen Padding: 15px on all sides
  • Card Padding: 15px internal
  • Border Radius - Cards: 12px
  • Border Radius - Buttons: 8px
  • Bottom Navigation Height: 70px
Animations
  • Screen Transitions: 300ms, ease-in-out
  • Button Hover: 200ms, ease
  • Progress Bar Fill: 500ms, ease-out
  • Modal Open/Close: 250ms
